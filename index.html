Instructions

  • Quizx is a fun game, where the player is asked to
    answer a bunch of general knowledge question to
    test their knowledge.

  • The player can choose between various
    difficulties.

  • Each question HAS a time limit of 10 seconds,
    by then the player should answer the
    question.

  • Lastly the score will be displayed based on
    the number of correct answer the players
    has given.
